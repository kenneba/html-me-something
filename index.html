Trip to ATL

A Town, Atlanta, The A; whatever you call it, I love it. Atlanta is one of my favirite cities on the East Coast. At one point in my life I planned to move to one of the suburbs but life happensa and I wasn't able to make the move. From June 19th to June 23rd, 2017, I took a trip to Atlanta, GA. I have been quite a few times growning up as I have relatives in the area. But this trip was by far my favorite. I took the trip as a celebration of my 30th birthday. And while many people prefer to take group trips, I made this trip alone.

June 19th

I left in my car to take that supposedly 8 hour trek to Atlanta. I left in the morning around 1o am and I made it to my hotel at around 6. I really enjoyed the drive.

June 20th

My first full day and I wasted no time. My hotel was in Norcross so I made it to downtown Atlanta in 20-30 minutes. There was hardly any traffic. I knew I wanted to get a CityPass and if you've never bought one, I recommend them. was decided to take a trip to the CNN Studios.

June 21st - My Birthday!

I started decided to go to 2 attractions on my birthday, World of Coca Cola and Georgia Aquarium. If you're not familiar with the locations they are walking distance away from each other.I started at the Coca Cola factory.
